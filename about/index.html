Jina AI is a Neural Search Company 

Jina is building an open-source neural search ecosystem, letting developers and enterprises build search systems that are fast, scalable, and work with any kind of data.

Join us!

Our Commitments

Community
We nurture a diverse and welcoming community. We love to share our knowledge and support community members to use our products in millions of different ways. Your feedback means everything!
Open Source
We are evangelists of open source and believe it's the best approach to make artificial intelligence truly accessible.
AI in production
We are building the biggest neural search ecosystem. Jina enables developers to use deep-learning models to tackle real-world problems in a fast, scalable, and stable fashion.

Why we started Jina

Why we started Jina

Deep learning technology is transforming how we interact with big data, whether for scientific applications, consumer software, or internal enterprise systems.

We started Jina AI to create a solution ready to keep up with the emerging needs for all kinds of search. With the growing need to process huge quantities of data, Jina helps you stay on top of the ever-changing data landscape.
