Introducing Rationale: a Decision-Making Tool Powered by Latest GPT and In-Context Learning

With the incredible capabilities of generative AI, 2023 is shaping up to be a game-changing year for startups and builders. Don’t just take our word for it; try Rationale out for yourself and see the difference it can make for your business. Get started today!

Jina AI
January 02, 2023 • 4 minutes read

Today, we're thrilled to announce Rationale, an AI-powered decision-making tool that's here to help business owners, managers, and individuals make tough decisions with confidence.

Decision-making can be a daunting task, especially when you have to weigh multiple factors and consider the potential consequences of your choices. That's where Rationale comes in. With state-of-the-art GPT3.x and in-context learning algorithms, our app is specifically designed to help you make informed decisions.

Pros & Cons listing & SWOT analysis

One of the standout features of Rationale is its ability to generate cost-benefit analyses, pros and cons lists, SWOT analyses, and other decision-making techniques in seconds. This means you can quickly and easily weigh your options and make a more rational decision.

Personalized analysis

But that's not all. Rationale is also personalized to you and your company, taking into account relevant information to deliver more accurate analysis and better inform your decisions. Plus, you can customize the tone of the analysis to align with your preferred narrative, making it more relatable and effective.

And the best part? Our AI is constantly learning from your decisions, which means it will get more accurate and personalized over time. So not only will you be able to make better decisions with Rationale, but the app will also get better at helping you make those decisions.

Multilingual support

One of the key features of Rationale is its multilingual support. Not only is the user interface available in multiple languages, but the algorithm itself is designed to provide analysis in any language that you input. This means you can get high-quality, accurate analysis in your preferred language, allowing for even more comprehensive decision-making.

Universal experience on every device

One of the great things about Rationale is that it's built as a progressive web app (PWA), which means it can be used on both phones and desktop browsers with a native app-like user experience. This makes it easy and convenient to use, no matter where you are or what device you have.

To use Rationale as a PWA, simply visit our website on your phone or desktop browser and follow the prompts to install the app. It's just like installing a native app, but without the need to visit an app store.

And if you prefer a traditional native app experience, don't worry – we'll be launching iOS and Android apps in the near future. Keep an eye out for updates, and be sure to download the app when it becomes available.

Summary

So, if you're someone who values their time and wants to make the best decisions possible, give Rationale a try. With fast, high-quality analysis reports and the ability to access the app in your preferred language, it's never been easier to make informed, rational decisions. And with API access to our AI engine, you can even export your data and integrate it into your own applications.

We're excited to see how Rationale can help you make smarter, more confident decisions. Give it a try today and see for yourself the power of AI-assisted decision-making.
