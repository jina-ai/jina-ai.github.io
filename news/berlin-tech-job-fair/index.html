Jina AI is happy to announce that on the 10th of November we will participate in the Berlin Tech Job Fair. The job fair provides a great opportunity to find or explore a new career in engineering and open source. You'll also have the chance to learn from the top experts in the IT/digital industries and network with peers.

Learn more about the Fair below:

If you join, come by our booth and ask us anything you want. Our team members will be happy to answer your questions in regard to open job positions, working culture, flexible working hours, remote work settings, and anything you want to know about us and our team!

Alongside all of this, we're happy to share the benefits of working at Jina and what it is like to be part of our multicultural diverse team.

We're also glad to discuss a range of things, such as our different products, our contributions to the open-source community, the company's background, and how we built our company up in the midst of the pandemic.
