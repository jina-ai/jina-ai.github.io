Rationale 0.4 Update

Introducing Rationale 0.4 - the ultimate decision-making tool now with personalized profiles! Create multiple profiles, input your backstory and context information, and get accurate analysis tailored to your background.

Engineering Group
January 16, 2023 • 2 minutes read

Rationale is a decision-making AI powered by the latest version of GPT-3 and in-context learning. It assists business owners, managers, and individuals in making tough decisions by generating a pros and cons list, SWOT analysis, and multi-option analysis. By considering all relevant factors, Rationale helps users make rational decisions.

💡
Please force refresh the browser to see the latest change. This can be usually achieved by Hold ⇧ Shift (MacOS) or Ctrl (Windows/Linux) and click the Reload button.

New Profiles Feature

Our latest release features the brand new profiles feature, allowing users to create their own personalized profiles for even more accurate decision analysis.

With the new profiles feature, users can now create multiple profiles and use them in their decision analysis. This allows for a high level of customization, as users can input arbitrary text as background and context information for their analysis. This greatly improves the accuracy and relevance of the analysis results, ensuring that users are making informed decisions based on their backstory and circumstances.

Previous versions of Rationale only allowed for restricted questionnaire information to be input into the decision analysis. With the new profiles feature, users can now tailor the analysis to their unique background and context, resulting in more accurate and actionable results.

Free users are allowed to use 1 profile, which can be modified, updated, or deleted at any time. Lite subscribers can have access to 3 profiles, standard subscribers can have access to 10 profiles and max subscribers can have access to 50 profiles. This allows users to tailor their analysis to different scenarios and contexts, depending on their subscription level.

In addition to the new profiles feature, we've made several other improvements and bug fixes to ensure that you have the best possible experience when using Rationale. Upgrade to Rationale 0.4 today and take control of your decision-making process with a personalized, accurate analysis.
