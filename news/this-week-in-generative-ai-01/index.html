August 03, 2023 • 2 minutes read

PromptPerfect - Elevate your prompts to perfection

Taming OpenAI's Function Call

We’re excited to introduce a new function debugger that seamlessly integrates with OpenAI’s Function Calling API. This new feature offers auto-generated JSON schemas and the ability to test prompts across multiple functions with traceability. This testbed allows you to check how your functions respond to natural language prompts, making it easier for you to ensure that your functions are working properly.

Claude 2 and Baidu ERNIE integration

We’ve added the Claude 2 and Baidu ERNIE models to PromptPerfect, so you can utilize them on the optimizer, streamline, few-shot, and prompt-as-service pages.

Manage your subscriptions better

We recently added a ‘Manage subscription’ button on top of the billing page for your convenience. You can now easily manage your subscription plans through this new feature.

SceneXplain - Explore Image storytelling beyond pixels

Read textual images in their original language

We’ve added a new feature that lets you read textual images in their original language. This feature helps you to preserve the context's meaning. For example, translating the word "可口可樂" to English may result in it losing its meaning.
