Jina AI Cloud: Production-ready Scalable Multimodal AI

Jina AI Cloud is the most advanced MLOps platform for building multimodal AI applications in the cloud. Users can translate their data and a few lines of code into a production-ready service without dealing with infrastructure complexity or scaling hassles.

Leon Wolf, Scott Martens
May 09, 2023 • 2 minutes read

We are excited to announce the launch of Jina AI Cloud: The central place for creating, launching, and managing neural search, creative AI, and multimodal AI applications.

Jina AI Cloud is a platform for quick access to all the information you need to develop, adapt and integrate AI into your business. It provides an intuitive and easy-to-use experience with accessible onboarding while letting experienced users efficiently set up, monitor, and manage AI services.

With Jina AI Cloud, you can access real-time logs, Grafana dashboards, and detailed itemization of resource usage for your services. The platform anchors Jina AI’s entire ecosystem of tools to accelerate AI application development.

Features:

  • Build out-of-the-box multimodal search applications.
  • Offload costly AI hosting and maintenance to our Inference service
  • Fine-tune AI models to maximize quality
  • View detailed information about application performance with Jina AI Flows
  • Direct support via live chat

Our flexible pricing system scales smoothly with your demand, so you only pay for the resources you need. With our business-friendly Pro plan, you use as many credits as you need and receive an invoice at the end of each month.

If you have a high-demand service, contact us about our Enterprise plan, which offers tailored solutions and volume discounts!

If you want to try Jina AI Cloud out to see if it’s for you, our Free plan provides enough access to experiment, learn and create prototype applications at your own pace without paying anything!

At Jina AI, we are committed to making multimodal AI accessible to everyone, not just AI experts and people with expensive hardware. Jina AI Cloud enables that by providing users with an intuitive, empowering, and reasonably priced cloud platform for building multimodal AI applications.

For more information, visit our website at https://cloud.jina.ai/.
