Jina AI’s PyCon US 2023 Ticket Giveaway Challenge

We’re giving away chances to win full conference passes to PyCon US, valued at 750 USD
Jocelyn Matthews • 2 minutes read

You could win a full conference pass for PyCon US!

Jina AI is a Partner sponsor at PyCon’s 20th anniversary event this April 19-27 in Salt Lake City, Utah. To celebrate, we’re giving away chances to win full conference passes to the event, valued at 750 USD. Winners will also receive a cool Jina t-shirt from our swag store and have the opportunity to meet our engineer Johannes Messner, a featured speaker at the event.

We want these tickets and tees to go to our most diehard advocates and our emerging community members - so we’ve created some flexible ways to express your enthusiasm for Jina - just share your best Jina social post or pledge to volunteer in our booth. Our favorite entrants will receive full conference passes to PyCon US.

Enter here!

The deadline for this giveaway is 11:59 pm Pacific time, March 29, 2023.

Winners will be notified by March 30, 2023, and Jina will supply winners with a discount code for a free ticket.

Can’t make it to Salt Lake City, Utah? No worries! We know our community is global, and we’ve got you covered. Stay tuned for our next giveaway, featuring five online passes to the event.

About PyCon 2023

“By the community, for the community”

PyCon US is the largest and longest-running annual gathering for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language.

At PyCon US 2023, you will find an amazing program filled with pre-conference tutorials and sponsor presentations, 90+ of the community’s best talks, which includes the Charlas track, brilliant keynote speakers, a lively Expo Hall filled with incredible Sponsors’ booths, and PyCon's famed lightning talks on each main conference day. Following the conference days, there are four days of sprints that are free and open to the public, and offer an opportunity for anyone to collaborate and contribute to a project, even if it is their first time.

PyCon US offers a whole host of events such as the Job Fair, Summits, Open Spaces, and PyLadies Auction, and don’t forget about the “hallway” track, which brings together Python users from around the world.

PyCon US 2023 Dates

  • Tutorials - April 19-20, 2023
  • Sponsor Presentations - April 20, 2023
  • Opening Reception April 20, 2023
  • Main Conference and Online - April 21-23, 2023
  • Job Fair - April 23, 2022
  • Sprints - April 24-27, 2023
