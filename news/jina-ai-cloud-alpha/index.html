Jina AI Cloud Alpha Launch

Announcing the alpha launch of Jina AI Cloud, designed to streamline and simplify deployment and management of your multimodal applications

Engineering Group
January 24, 2023 • 3 minutes read

Jina AI is all about building an open-source ecosystem around every task related to multimodal AI and AI-powered search. We’re happy to announce our alpha launch of Jina AI Cloud, designed to streamline and simplify deployment and management of your multimodal applications. Now you can spend more time building amazing products instead of getting bogged down in Kubernetes, deployments, and all those other nuts and bolts.

Activity at a Glance with the Dashboard

The new dashboard shows you all activity at a glance, giving a clear understanding of the status of your Flows, Apps and deployments. Get an overview of important metrics such as the number of requests served, active Flows, and recently completed Finetuner runs. You can also quickly identify any potential issues or areas that require attention.

Deploy Multimodal Search Apps

With this release, you can create awesome multi-modal search applications with just a few clicks! Upload your dataset, choose the fields you want to search and filter, and voilà, you’ve got a production-ready search application that you can integrate into your business using RESTful APIs. Try it out and tell us what you think!

On-Demand AI Models with Inference API

The Inference API takes care of all your AI model hosting and deployment needs. Just choose the state-of-the-art model for your task and start sending queries! No more painful GPU costs or wondering how you can optimize and finetune your CLIP deployment.

Right now, the Inference API offers access to a variety of CLIP model variations. Going further, we plan to expand our offering beyond CLIP and let you offload all your model-hosting requirements to us! Stay tuned for more news in the coming weeks (or subscribe to our newsletter!)

Streamlined Finetuning via Finetuner API

Easily enhance pre-trained models with Jina AI's Finetuner and boost your performance with a low budget and few training samples - all in a simple yet powerful platform. With Jina AI Cloud, you can view, manage and create Finetuner runs directly in the browser.

You can not only track all your previous runs with detailed information, but also see how to implement the resulting model.

In Finetuner, you can easily configure hyper-parameters to increase the accuracy of the model. Finetuner is perfect for neural model task optimization, allowing you to get the best results with the least amount of effort.

Monitor JCloud Flows

Our CLI tool jc is great to get information about running Flows in JCloud (Jina AI’s cloud hosting service), but the command line can be a bit limiting at times.

The Jina AI Cloud platform lets you navigate between Flows and see all details quickly. You can also monitor dashboards and logs directly in the UI, to make sure everything is going as planned.

Manage your DocArrays

DocArray is an open source multimodal data storage library that lets you store all kinds of data in a unified format. Use the storage view to view and share your DocArrays, and then use them in all our services. DocArray is the perfect way to pass data through the Jina AI ecosystem.

Manage Personal Access Tokens

Personal access tokens make the Jina AI World go round. Use these tokens to access the Jina AI Cloud via your code, authenticate your applications and utilize our great features!

Built-in Chat Support

With our integrated chat, you can get direct help regarding any issues or questions about the Jina AI Cloud or ecosystem.

Get Started Now

Looking to build your search solution? Get started today with Jina AI and try our services for free!
