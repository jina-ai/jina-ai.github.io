How Can I Contribute To Jina?

Make Jina better!
Are you an experienced Python developer and wanna make great impacts to Jina Core?
Check #good-first-issue or #help-wanted in our Jina Box or Jina Console repos
And get highlighted as a contributor in our regular release
Hone your technical writing skills
Do you enjoy writing technical content that educates other developers?
Improve Jina's README or docs
Put your Jina experience in a blog post and share with us by tagging @JinaAI_ on Twitter
Help others learn Jina
Do you like to share your knowledge and help others get up to speed?
Host a meetup to give a talk on Jina
Create videos to teach people about Jina
Write a blog post to share your learnings
Share your Jina projects
Built an interesting project with Jina? Inspire others by sharing your work!
Make sure your project is on GitHub and Jina is included in the dependencies
Share your project in #showcase channel of our community slack , and we’ll share with the community
Integrate an awesome AI model
Noticed some AI models are missing on Jina Hub? Developed your own deep-learning models and want to share them with others?
Write your own Executor
Add your Executor to Jina Hub
Become a front-end friend
More into front-end development? You can be our front-end hero for JinaJS or Jina Console!
Check for #good-first-issue labels in our JinaJS or Jina Console repos
Let us know how you’d like to improve by submitting pull requests
If successful, you’ll get highlighted as a contributor in our monthly release
